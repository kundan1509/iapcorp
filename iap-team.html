About

IAP Company (IAP) Pvt. Ltd. is a 20 year old organization with interests in IT Solutions, products and services, Media, E-tailing and E-governance. It began as a software development company with a single point focus on Japanese IT Market, and over the period it expanded its horizons and area of operations but with a clear agenda to be successful and a leader in all its business.
