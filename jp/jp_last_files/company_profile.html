Company Profile

IAP Company (IAP) Pvt. Ltd. is a software products and services company with a Japanese genesis catering to a large customer base of customers in Japan & India offering them IT solutions and adding immense value to their business.

The extensive software development capabilities including software design, development, deployment, and support functions makes IAP a key player in IT outsourcing from India. A strong workforce of 1000+ has an impeccable track record of delivering software projects and IT solutions totaling over 60,000 person months to top notch corporate, organizations in Japan and India including Fortune 500 companies, government and semi governmental organizations.

It is one of the fastest growing companies in the end-to-end e-governance space in India. A global company with a strong local presence, today it is delivering solutions to100+ central and state government agencies in India.

With IPR ownership of bouquet of home grown software products, the products can be customized and delivered to a client's satisfaction in variety of delivery models. The company also offers embedded solutions in telecom sector and has powered some of the latest 3G handsets rolled out in the Japanese market.

The world's largest and India's leading Citizen Journalism news platform, www.merinews.com, is powered by IAP's in-house technology team.

Vision

Envision becoming India's leading technology driven, and service provider company. To ensure that its solutions give maximum value to the customer's operations and product development.

Mission

Has a mission to provide solutions, services and software products through consistent use of cutting-edge technologies, and highest standards of quality and management systems.
Develop world-class software systems and offer unique SAAS models - for wide range of industries in India and globally. Bringing value to stakeholders and shareholders through deploying ICT solutions in the areas of e-governance, rural development and training and work towards continuous social development.
