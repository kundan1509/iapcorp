Leadership

Vipul Kant Upadhyay

Chief Executive Officer & Managing Director

He is the Founder & CEO of IAP Co. Pvt. Ltd. (www.iapcorp.com). He is also the founder of www.merinews.com : India's 1st & the largest Citizen generated news platform. In his young age he has garnered valuable experience by working closely with various mega Japanese corporate like Sojitz Corporation (formerly called NISSO IWAI), Mitsui Group, NEC Group, Nikko Cordial Group, Pasona Group. (Japan.) etc. and various government departments & entities from India and Japan. Vipul has served in senior positions of several Japanese companies' India operations and steered their operations in India. He is a member of the Executive Board of India Japan Global Partnership Summit 2011 and also founding member of India Center Foundation. He graduated as a bachelor of Physics and Mathematics from the University of Allahabad. Thereafter he completed his Masters in International Business from Symbiosis University, Pune in the year 1998 with specialization in international finance. Vipul has been key note speaker at various national & international forums including OCED SME Conference (Tokyo), FIEO Seminar (New Delhi), AMIC Annual Conference (Manila & Brisbane), Asian Digital Conference (Singapore), Supercomm India (New Delhi) to name a few. Various magazines in India and abroad including Dataquest, PC Quest, etc have published articles written by Vipul.
