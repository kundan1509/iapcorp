iArticle Pro is an efficient process automation and management tool for "content creation and publication business" that includes -

  • Article & Writer Management
  • Content Development in various formats Text, Images and Videos.
  • Content Publication

iArticle Pro – A tool built keeping in mind the growing digital media sector around the globe and the need to automate the writer management and publication process for news agencies, publication houses, companies who provide content as a service etc.

iArticle Pro helps to manage and keep track of all writers, their bills, their productivity etc. all at one place with less time and maximum efficiency.

It provides the capability to publish content/articles in attractive digital formats across multi properties and multi channels from a single point.

Whether you’re a Writer, Editor or a Publisher iArticle Pro has the best in class customizable platform to help drive your content strategy forward.

iArticle Pro offers quick article writing and publishing features. The product has intuitive, one screen interface which is easy to use and makes article creation easier than ever before.

Features –

Writer Management - If You Outsource your Writing Process or if you have a Group of Writers, Editors & Publishers, iArticle Pro helps to manage and keep track of all writers, their bills, their productivity etc. all at one place with less time and maximum efficiency.

Assign tasks to different users (writers, editors, publishers) and get the summary on dashboard for easy and quick reach. Get notified on your mobile for any assigned tasks and its completion.

Article Management - Create, Submit and Publish Article, Provision to schedule article for future date publication, assign article to category’s expert writers, Permit user to list article as per defined category, location, topic and tag, Review submitted articles; ‘Reject’ or ‘Approve & Publish’ articles.

Multi-Channel & Multi Property Publishing - iArticle Pro provides content delivery across different channels or migrate approved content into existing CMS to go live across different platforms. iArticle Pro can be facilitated and tailored as per the existing WCM environment.

Publish content on multiple websites/properties from the same control panel. We provide integration with popular CMS’s (WordPress, Drupal, Joomla, Magento, Open Cart) and various other content platforms to connect your existing content with ease.

Billing Management - iArticle Pro’s in-built billing management panel helps you streamline cost calculations for the Writers, Editors wages. You can view the expenditure on particular writers, editors or for the project on the whole for previous months, present months which helps you manage your investment indirectly.

It helps you automate your billing process by providing you reports and cost calculations on per word, per article written/reviewed or you can provide wages to writers & editors on per month basis.

Multilingual Interface - Localized into different languages, Select the language you need to reach your local audience with just a click! Yes, it’s that simple just click the button and select the required language to change the complete layout in the language you want.

Plagiarism Checker - Integrated tool that intelligently checks your text for similarities against billions of sources. Scan billions of internet pages to check for plagiarized content and provide unique content to the users. Get detailed reports about the plagiarized content.

Kindly visit www.iarticlepro.com to explore more about iArticle Pro.
