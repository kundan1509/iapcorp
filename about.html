Welcome to IAP Group

IAP is an Information, Communication & Technology (ICT) driven business group with interests in the field of

  • Information Technology
  • Rollout Services
  • Media
  • E-tailing (read- retail + ecommerce)

INNOVATION, ACTION AND PERFORMANCE are the pillars of IAP’s business ETHOS and DNA. These three words together reflect the DNA of IAP.

The journey of IAP started two decades ago as a software development company focused on the Japanese IT outsourcing market. Over the years, IAP broadened the business horizon with an aim to build successful businesses at the same time contributing as a responsible organization to build a better society.

Infotech : draws its strength from the experience of catering to large customer base in Japan, India and across the globe. Its seasoned team of 1000+ engineers with experience on wide variety of technologies, domains and verticals gives unique technical prowess.

IAP Infotech offers wide array of outsourcing services including Software Development, IT Infrastructure Management (IMS), IT Staffing Services in addition to a range of home grown proprietary products & solutions.

Rollout Services : is a division of IAP Company Pvt. Ltd. offering unique expertise of implementing, managing and delivering ICT based projects which require last mile / dispersed / distributed / remote / multi-location project management, supervision, whether on field or within office premises. IAP rollout team has successfully implemented various large and mid-sized projects for various government and non-government organizations – including but not limited to enrolments, IT system setup and management, hand holding, capacity building etc. IAP’s team has project rollout capabilities across the length and breadth of India including the far flung rural areas of every state and union territories.

Media: iArticle Pro an efficient process automation and management tool for "content creation and publication business"

A tool built keeping in mind the growing digital media sector around the globe and the need to automate the writer management and publication process for news agencies, publication houses, companies who provide content as a service etc.

iArticle Pro helps to manage and keep track of all writers, their bills, their productivity etc. all at one place with less time and maximum efficiency. It provides the capability to publish content/articles in attractive digital formats across multi properties and multi channels from a single point.

E-tailing : Ecos is a web based unified platform which offers the ability to do business directly with consumers (B2C) and with businesses (B2B) It is a Customizable, scalable and high availability solution built on the Java - Java EE platform using open standards.

Working Models

  • Franchise Based (B2B)
  • End user based (B2C)
